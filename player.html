How to read cards

R = Red
G = Green
B = Blue
Y = Yellow
W = Wild (any color)
🔢 Numbers = match number or color
⛔ X = Skip next player
🔁 R = Reverse turn order
➕2 = Next player draws 2 cards
🌈 WW = Wild, pick any color
